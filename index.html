About

Sky Cafe, is one of Philly’s oldest Indonesian establishments and a fixture of South Philly’s diverse neighborhood.

It has been owned and operated by chef Lily Tjia, her charming daughter, Betty Yu, and Yu's husband, Anto Lys since 2010.
